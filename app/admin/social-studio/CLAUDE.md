### Typography
- **Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts, weights 300–700)
- **Style:** -0.01em letter spacing, tight headlines (1.1–1.2 line height)
- **Hero:** 72–80px, weight 800–900
- **Body:** 16–18px, weight 400–500

### Visual Style
- **Liquid Glass Corporativo** — Frosted glass with backdrop blur
- **Dark Navy Engineering** — Deep navy backgrounds, NOT pure black
- **Futuristic Minimalism** — Clean, precise, technical
- **Aurora/Gradient** — Blue to cyan transitions
- **Glassmorphism** — Cards with 24px radius, glass borders, blue glows
- **NO stock photos** — Icons, gradients, UI mockups only
- **NO people/faces** in generated images

## Brand Voice

- **Language:** Spanish (Costa Rica) — direct, no fluff
